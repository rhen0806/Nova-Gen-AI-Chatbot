# Nova-Gen-AI-Chatbot
A course project wherein we developed an AI chatbot using Python programming language and APIs.

The Nova Generative Artificial Intelligence (Gen-AI) chatbot is an advanced artificial intelligence (AI) system designed to enhance human-computer interactions through intelligent responses and automation. Built using cutting-edge machine learning models, Nova Gen-AI leverages the application programming interfaces (APIs) to integrate with powerful AI engines like Gemini, thus enabling seamless natural language understanding. 

Whether used for answering queries, generating content, or assisting with complex tasks, Nova Gen-AI provides an intuitive and efficient experience with its ability to learn and adapt, representing a step forward in the evolution of AI-driven technology.

Thus, the Nova Gen-AI chatbot is built to simplify tasks, improve efficiency, and deliver
an efficient user experience.

Functions
  The Nova Gen-AI chatbot has a wide range of functions due to being a large, multi-modal
  model. Those functions are as follows, but are not limited to :

  a. Natural Language Processing (NLP), which includes;
    ● Text generation and translation
    ● Question answering and summarization
    ● Sentiment analysis and emotion recognition
    ● Named entity recognition and relationship extraction
  
  b. Computer Vision, which includes:
    ● Image classification, object detection, and segmentation
    ● Facial recognition and emotion detection
    ● Image editing and enhancement
    
  c. Audio Processing, which includes:
    ● Speech recognition and generation
    ● Speaker identification and language identification
    ● Audio classification and transcription
  
  d. Machine Learning, which includes:
    ● Supervised and unsupervised learning
    ● Model training and evaluation
    ● Predictive analytics and forecasting
  
  e. Data Analysis, which includes:
    ● Data cleaning and preprocessing
    ● Exploratory data analysis and visualization
    ● Statistical modeling and hypothesis testing
  
  Nova’s other capabilities include, but are not limited to: (f) knowledge base and information retrieval, (g) contextual understanding and reasoning, (h) problem-solving and decision-making, and (i) code generation and debugging.
  
  However, Nova still has its limitations, as one cannot fully replicate the wonder that is the human brain through artificial means. The Nova Gen-AI Chatbot’s limitations may include: (1) the lack of common sense knowledge and the ability to make intuitive judgments, (2) the lack of creativity in generating original ideas, (3) the lack of access to real-time information, as its knowledge cutoff was from as far back as April 2023, and (4) the exhibition of biases in its responses due to the training data it was fed on.

Beneficiaries

  Being a Gen-AI chatbot, Nova does not have a specified sample of users like social
  media platforms or online forums might have. It was designed to interact with a wide range of users,
  from individuals seeking information or assistance to researchers and developers exploring the
  capabilities of artificial intelligence (AI).
  
  The intended users of the Nova Gen-AI Chatbot are the following, but not limited to:
  
  a. Individuals seeking information or assistance
      These users may have questions about a wide range of topics, such as current events, weather, sports, or travel. They may also be looking for help with tasks        such as setting alarms, making appointments, or finding local businesses.
    
  b. Developers and researchers
      These users are interested in exploring the capabilities of AI and developing new applications for chatbot technology. They may ask questions about the             underlying algorithms and techniques used by chatbots, or they may provide feedback and suggestions for improvements.
  
  c. Students and educators
      These users use chatbots for educational purposes, such as getting help with homework, practicing language skills, or learning about new subjects.
  
  d. Business people and organizations
      These users use chatbots to provide customer support, answer frequently asked questions, or automate tasks such as scheduling appointments or processing            orders.
  
  Overall, the Nova Gen-AI Chatbot is designed to be a versatile and accessible tool for a wide range of users, striving to provide helpful and informative responses to its beneficiaries.
